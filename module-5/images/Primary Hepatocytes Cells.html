Achieve a reliable, high-quality supply of primary hepatocytes

Gain peace of mind and protect your client commitments by delivering consistent, high-quality, plateable hepatocytes at a fraction of the cost.

Our unique team brings together experts with backgrounds in organ recovery, perfusion, transplantation, research and hepatocyte isolation. With these combined skillsets, Organ Perfusion and Cellular Services has revolutionized the organ recovery to cryopreserved hepatocyte process to accomplish a lower frequency of failed isolations and industry-leading yields per human and rodent.

Advantages to an augmented isolation team approach

  • Advanced Donor Screening

    With extensive experience in donor management for organ transplantation, our team is expertly trained in advanced donor screening techniques that help identify donors who would traditionally be declined.

  • Pre-Isolation Protocols for Higher Quality Hepatocytes

    Our proven pre-isolation protocols ensure that we consistently deliver human organs that are properly flushed and have pristine vasculature.

  • Optimized Yields

    Make backorders a thing of the past. Our optimized yields per human and rodent produce unprecedented lot sizes, ensuring reliable and continuous research support for your clients.

  • Strong OPO + Transplant Center Relationships

    Our team’s strong relationships with OPOs and transplant centers ensure a consistent supply of healthy and diseased cells.
